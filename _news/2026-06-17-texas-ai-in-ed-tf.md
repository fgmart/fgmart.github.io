This spring I was part of the Texas AI in (K-12) Education Task Force,
convened by the Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC) and led by Ryan
Torbey, PhD, Carol Fletcher, PhD, Sofia De Jesus, M.S., and Allen
Antoine, M.Ed.

Our findings are now published! We advise that Texas:


1. Establish state leadership;
1. Issue statewide guidance;
1. Fund professional development; and
1. Update student learning standards.

Read the white paper
[here](https://tacc.utexas.edu/education/educators/align/).
